Neuron-O

background knowledge

Neurons are a special type of cell inside of our body. Small as they are, billions of neurons organized logistically form the complex neuron network that support our daily thinking, moving and feeling. Because of their great significance, scientists are working hard to figure out the complete picture of neurons. On this basis, some neurological diseases that endanger human health are expected to be resolved.

design intention

Below is a set of stickers based on a imaginery character, named Neuron-O. In a cartoon way, I want to express my attraction for those tiny creatures and hope more people can get to know more about them like I do. Some of the stickers refer to the characteristics of neurons themselves, such as signal transmission and axonotmesis.

You can get this collection from WeChat sticker gallery. Please use “神经元” as key words to search.
