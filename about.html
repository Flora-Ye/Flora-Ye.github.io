about

Hi, my name is Flora. I am interested in biomedical visualization. In other words, I want to tell a good story of things that happen in organisms. I start drawing and painting since I was a little kid. In China, parents usually send their kids to all kinds of classes when they are very young, which may not be the real hobby of them. However, things seem a little different for me. I had a perfect art teacher, who didn’t judge students’ work with stupid dogma, but always encouraged us to think creatively, even if we were just kids at that time. From then on, drawing has accompanied me to walk through the whole life journey. I am also a person who loves science. I need to thank my father for that. He is an engineer, and he is brilliant. He told me that he got all A in science and math during high school. (I believe him because he also admitted that he failed several times in English class.) He always told me to ask more questions when I was learning new things. It turned out to match with the idea that my teacher taught me later on, critical thinking.
